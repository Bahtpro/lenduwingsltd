About Us

  • Who We Are

    Lendu Wings Consultancy Ltd, headquartered in Mbale, Kenya, and registered under the Companies Act (Cap 486, Number 17 of 2015), is a premier consultancy firm specializing in providing insightful and innovative solutions across diverse fields. Our strength lies in the exceptional expertise of our founding members, each bringing a unique blend of academic excellence, consultancy and research prowess, and practical experience. We are dedicated to delivering exceptional services tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients. Our firm places a high value on client satisfaction, confidentiality, and professionalism, ensuring that every project we undertake is approached with a commitment to excellence and a focus on delivering comprehensive and informed solutions. We specialize in consultancy services focused on providing investment management and strategic financial advice, capacity building and development and offering tailored solutions to individuals and institutional clients either independently or in collaboration with other experts to meet our clients' specific needs. Our commitment to delivering high-quality services underscores our emphasis on client satisfaction, confidentiality, and professionalism.

  • What We Do

    Financial analysis, Credit analysis, administration and delinquency management: Covering financial analysis, credit analysis, administration, and delinquency management. Entrepreneurship and Financial management training. Training of lenders and borrowers. Strategic plans/Business plan/proposal writing/Report writing. Policy recommendation. Data analysis SPSS, STATA, MINITAB and E-VIEWS. Training in research methods/capacity building. Project Management/Project Monitoring & Evaluation. Public Policy Review and Analysis. Social-economic Research and data collection. Risk assessment and Environmental Impact Assessment. Trade and investment.

  • Our Mission

    Joab is currently the Founder and President at Joab’s Capital & Equity Research; a hedge fund and family office advisory firm registered in St. Nevis and Kitts tax haven that specializes in providing investment management and strategic financial advice to wealthy individuals and institutional clients, leveraging the favourable tax regulations and privacy benefits of the jurisdiction. JC&ER offers tailored services to optimize tax efficiency, asset protection, and wealth preservation. Joab is also an Advisory Board Member at Africa Trade and Investment Global Summit (ATIGS) a unique high-level conference and exhibition, prestigious, biennial business platform designed specifically to promote and facilitate international trade between Americas, Asia, EU, UAE, with Africa, facilitate foreign direct investment in Africa and provide a platform for businesses to expand into new markets. Joab is also a Cofounder at The Baileys and Partners Newsletter, an insightful financial newsletter targeting UHNWIs, sovereign wealth funds, hedge funds and other asset managers. Joab is a holder of B.Sc. in Applied Statistics from Maseno University majoring in Econometrics and Actuarial Science. He also holds an APID Diploma from London School and M.Sc. in Software Engineering from Kansas University. Joab is also a Certified International Investment Analyst (CIIA).
