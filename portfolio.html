Personal Profile

  • Managing Director

    Dr. Geoffrey Ligare Abung’ana

    Geoffrey is a founding member of Lendu Wings Consultancy Ltd. He is also a founding member of Rich Grain Millers Ltd (a maize flour manufacturing company). He is a lecturer of Economics at Kaimosi Friends University and Maseno University in Kenya. He is a holder of BA Economics, Mathematics and Sociology (Egerton University), Master of Arts in Economics from Maseno Univeristy and currently undertaking a PhD Economics degree from Maseno University. He also holds a PhD in Theology from Northwestern Christian University. He has vast experience from the banking sector (Kenya commercial bank and Equity Bank (K) Ltd) where he worked as a credit officer and administrator. He has also worked in the microfinance sector (Opportunity Kenya Microfinance) as a Branch Manager where he gained experience in credit administration and training of teams. He is an experienced and keen to detail researcher in economics and social research. Dr. Abung’ana has vast knowledge in negotiations, linkages and partnerships.

  • Chief Finance Officer

    Dr. Evans Ovamba Kiganda

    Evans, a distinguished Senior Lecturer in the Department of Economics at Maseno University, brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to Lendu Wings Consultancy Ltd. With an academic journey marked by notable achievements, Evans holds a PhD in Economics, an MA in Economics, and a BA in Economics and Mathematics. His commitment to education and research is evident from his foundational role as the Chairperson of the Department of Economics at Kaimosi Friends University and his extensive teaching career spanning over eight years.A founding member of Lendu Wings Consultancy Ltd., Evans leverages his profound expertise to provide insightful and innovative consultancy services. His research prowess is well-documented through numerous publications in various international peer-reviewed journals, highlighting his contribution to the field of Economics. In addition to his academic and research accomplishments, Evans has substantial experience in the banking sector. Over the course of more than eight years, he served as a Treasury Economist and Credit Administrator, polishing his skills and deepening his understanding of financial systems. This diverse background enables Evans to offer a unique perspective and valuable insights to Lendu Wings Consultancy Ltd., ensuring comprehensive and informed solutions for clients.

  • Chief Investment Officer

    Mr. Joab Khamala

    Joab is currently the Founder and President at Joab’s Capital & Equity Research; a hedge fund and family office advisory firm registered in St. Nevis and Kitts tax haven that specializes in providing investment management and strategic financial advice to wealthy individuals and institutional clients, leveraging the favourable tax regulations and privacy benefits of the jurisdiction. JC&ER offers tailored services to optimize tax efficiency, asset protection, and wealth preservation. Joab is also an Advisory Board Member at Africa Trade and Investment Global Summit (ATIGS) a unique high-level conference and exhibition, prestigious, biennial business platform designed specifically to promote and facilitate international trade between Americas, Asia, EU, UAE, with Africa, facilitate foreign direct investment in Africa and provide a platform for businesses to expand into new markets. Joab is also a Cofounder at The Baileys and Partners Newsletter, an insightful financial newsletter targeting UHNWIs, sovereign wealth funds, hedge funds and other asset managers. Joab is a holder of B.Sc. in Applied Statistics from Maseno University majoring in Econometrics and Actuarial Science. He also holds an APID Diploma from London School and M.Sc. in Software Engineering from Kansas University. Joab is also a Certified International Investment Analyst (CIIA).
